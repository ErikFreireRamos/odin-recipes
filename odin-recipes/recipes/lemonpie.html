Lemon Pies

Ingredients

  • 200g of cornstarch biscuit
  • 150 of margarine
  • 1 can of condensed milk
  • 1 box of sour cream
  • juice of 4 lemons
  • zest of 2 lemons
  • 3 or 4 egg whites
  • 3 tablespoons of sugar
  • zest of 2 lemons to decorate

    • Steps

    1. Grind the cornstarch biscuit in a blender or food processor.
    2. Add the margarine and mix some more.
    3. Pour the dough into a form with a removable background (27 cm in diameter).
    4. With your hands, spread the crushed cookies on the bottom and sides of the form, covering the entire area evenly.
    5. Bake in a preheated oven (180Â°C) for approximately 10 minutes.
    6. Blend all ingredients in a blender (except lemon zest) until smooth and firm.
    7. Fill the already baked dough and take it to the fridge for 30 minutes.
    8. Beat the egg whites and add the sugar.
    9. Mix until you get a sigh point and take it to the oven until golden.
    10. Unmold the pie (without removing the false bottom), pour the frosting and add the lemon zest
